What?

TL;DR

Compose a tweet with the goal of getting the most bots or random people to favorite or retweet. The tweet is sent to a neutral twitter account, in order to level the playing ground.

Rules

  • 140 characters (duh)
  • Entries will be scored after 24 hours
  • Favorites are 1 point each
  • Retweets are awarded 2 points each
  • Bot follows will not be awarded points due to attribution difficulties

More Details

For the sake of transparency, the tweets are sent to this twitter account. Yes knowing this information means you could easily cheat, you cool hacker you, but don't be a donkus.
